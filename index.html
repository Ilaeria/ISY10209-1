About Me

I am currently studying towards a Bachelor of Information Technology through Southern Cross University, part-time through distance education while I work full-time. My major is Software Development and my current GPA is 6.33 (with 7 being the maximum available). I have received the WIT@SCU Excellence Award for both Applications Development and Principles of Programming. I hope to maintain this record and to receive the Recognition of Academic Excellence Award each year I study. I have a previous Bachelor of Arts degree from Victoria University of Wellington, with majors in English Literature and Classical Studies.

My full-time job is as the IT Project Coordinator for an online dating company. This is a varied and busy job in a fascinating industry. I have a strong background in customer experience, adminstration, and fraud analysis. I'm hoping that my studies will enable me to improve and expand my current role. I particularly enjoy learning about User Experience Design and would like to focus on this area.

I live with my husband and our three fur-babies (one dog, Phoebe, and two cats, Jeddah and Bubbles) on the Gold Coast, in a house that we recently had custom-built. I'm originally from New Zealand but have lived in Australia for over 10 years, and on the Gold Coast since 2006. I love the weather and the relaxed atmosphere on the Gold Coast, but I'm not a fan of all the dangerous animals in Australia.
